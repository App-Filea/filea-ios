In short: Holfy respects your privacy. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

1. Introduction

Holfy is a vehicle document management app designed with respect for your privacy as our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

Holfy operates on a "local-first" principle: all your data stays on your device.

2.1 Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored only on your iPhone:

  • Vehicle information (brand, model, mileage, license plate)
  • Documents and invoices (photos, PDFs, notes)
  • Expense history and statistics
  • App preferences (currency, units of measurement)

We have no access to this data.

2.2 Diagnostic Data (Firebase Crashlytics)

To improve app stability and performance, we use Firebase Crashlytics which collects anonymous technical data:

  • Crash reports: Technical logs when the app crashes
  • Performance data: Load times, memory usage, CPU
  • Device identifier: Anonymous UUID to group incidents from the same device

This data is anonymous, not linked to your identity, and used solely to fix bugs and improve performance.

3. Data Usage

Your local data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your vehicles and documents in the app
  • Calculate your statistics and expenses
  • Generate tracking charts

Diagnostic data is used to:

  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app stability
  • Optimize performance

4. Storage and Backup

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored in the app's sandbox folder on your iPhone. This data is protected by iOS security.

4.2 iCloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable iCloud backup on your device, your Holfy data may be included in that backup. This backup is:

  • Managed by Apple through your personal iCloud account
  • End-to-end encrypted
  • Accessible only by you

We have no access to your iCloud backups.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

Holfy does not share any personal data with commercial, advertising, or marketing third parties.

The only data shared is:

  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google): Anonymous crash reports to improve stability

6. Data Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • All data is stored in the app's secure sandbox
  • No data transmission to external servers (except anonymous crash reports)
  • No user account required = no password leak risk
  • Your documents never leave your device

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Export: You can export your data at any time
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app deletes all your local data
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats (JSON, PDF, images)

8. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained as long as the app is installed
  • Crash reports: Retained for 90 days by Firebase Crashlytics

9. Children's Data

Holfy does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. The app may be used by minors under parental or guardian supervision.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. In case of major changes, we will notify you via:

  • An in-app notification
  • Updating the date at the top of this page

We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

11. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@holfy-app.com
  • Website: https://app-filea.github.io/filea-ios/

12. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
